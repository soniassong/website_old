About Me

Hello! I am passionate about building products through human creativity, pioneering technology, and a data-driven approach.

My experience developing highly complex fintech products from concept to launch has equipped me with a deep set of skills as a product manager. Through driving innovation in mature markets, I’ve honed the ability to navigate highly sensitive customer journeys and leverage data to deliver personalized experiences. With extensive stakeholder and ecosystem collaboration, I also navigated challenging regulatory and compliance environments and developed efficiency in risk management and decision-making under uncertainty.

As an advocate for technology, my master’s degree in Data Science and Machine Learning from UC Berkeley has deepened my understanding of the AI/ML landscape and strengthened my engineering skills. I’m excited to be at the forefront of generative AI applications, actively driving its transformative impact as the technology reshapes industries.

Outside of work, I am a music enthusiast at my core. Growing up playing the piano and guitar has not only deepened my appreciation for musical notes but also fostered an eye for finding beauty in the arts. I carry this creative mindset into product development, where it sparks innovation in design and problem-solving.
